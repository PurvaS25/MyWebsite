About PCCOER

Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering & Research (PCCOE&R) was established in 2014 under the aegis of Pimpri Chinchwad Education Trust (PCET). Presently the Institute offers NBA accredited UG Programmes in four disciplines viz. Civil Engineering, Computer Engineering, E&TC Engineering, Mechanical Engineering and also offers Information Technology. PCCOE&R is affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU), Pune and is approved by A.I.C.T.E., New Delhi, Govt. of Maharashtra, D.T.E, Mumbai. Recently the Institute has been accredited with A++ grade by NAAC in the very first cycle of accreditation.
